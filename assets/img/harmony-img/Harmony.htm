About us

Our Story

Background

Beginning in the ready-made garment (RMG) industry in 1988, harmony went on to establish several industry leaders, including harmony Garments Ltd., harmony Industries Ltd., harmony Washworks Ltd., GDS Chemicals Ltd., and harmony Denims Ltd., positioning itself as one of the pioneers in RMG export. gaining a strong reputation as a significant supplier for some of the most well-known clothing companies in the USA and Europe.
Harmony has a long history in construction dating back to 1991. Throughout that time, it has worked on numerous projects both independently and with the HHL Group. The renowned Safura Tower, a 16-story commercial landmark in Banani, the 200,000 square feet multi-facility centrally air-conditioned International School Dhaka (ISD) at Bashundhara, the 125,000 square feet Delhi Public School at Uttara, and cutting-edge RMG factories like its 150,000 square feet harmony Industries Ltd, 150,000 square feet harmony Denims Ltd, and the 35,000 square feet harmony Washworks Ltd at Dhaka EPZ are just a few of the buildings it has constructed. Additionally, the group contributed to the building of Apollo Hospitals Dhaka, the nation's first multidisciplinary super-specialty hospital, which is 550,000 square feet in size.

Harmony Holdings Limited (HHL) was founded in 2005 with the goal of changing the lifestyle of city dwellers by offering contemporary, practical, and aesthetically pleasing living and working spaces that can only be compared to the most successful developers worldwide. SHL eventually left the RMG sector and dissolved its interests to pursue its passion for construction.

HHL goes beyond the typical scopes of property development and integrates the best the world has to offer in construction, believing that building structures is only one aspect of construction and not an art form. From that point on, SHL has become the nation's most reputable and rapidly expanding real estate developer.

Who we are

Our clients enjoy exclusive access to the finest luxury apartments and exquisite commercial spaces, all in prime locations throughout Dhaka city, thanks to our impressive portfolio of the most unique and selective developments in the country, our stellar reputation in the real estate market, and our enviable relationships.

Our commitment is to continue building a portfolio of opulent properties and providing buyers and investors with unmatched, unmatched by competitors service. The reason for our success is our unwavering dedication to maintaining impeccable standards and paying close attention to details.

Not only does SHL build beyond expectations, but it also sets standards for you, for us, and for others. For this reason, we are proud to state that "Setting Standards" is our driving philosophy..

Our Approach

This dream motivates him or her to progressively save the necessary funds, which is the first step towards turning this dream into reality. This unwavering quest to make such a dream a reality can continue from generation to generation. Is it really realised to what degree this "dream" is realised once a space is bought, though?

We know that moment is an important stepping-stone for you, and hence we at harmony Holdings have been preparing for just that. Our apartments, condominiums and commercial complexes compete with the best that the modern world has to offer. From temperature-controlled swimming pools to lush rooftop gardens and terraces, from state-of-the-art gymnasiums to spacious walkways and children’s play facilities, you’ll find the solution to your need for stylish urban living with us at harmony.

Even business becomes pleasure in our exquisite commercial spaces with central air-conditioning, triple height lobby areas, multilayered parking facilities, and much more. Show the world you’ve arrived, by owning your own little piece of luxury.

Our Logo

The shape of human hands when held with the palms facing each other is the inspiration behind the design of our logo.

Typically, when we hold something in our hands, it's usually something we value or something we care about. Similar to how we shield a candle that provides us with light from airborne threats by covering it with our palms. We pick up and hold a small bird that has fallen from its nest, shielding it with our hands. A sculptor uses his fingers and palms to shape his creations when creating a masterpiece. As a result, our logo captures the thought and passion we put into each and every one of our creations.

Moreover, it is a symbol that inspires us on a daily basis to be caring towards our clients and protect their interests by providing an uncompromising level of service and superior products.

Vision, Mission & Values

VISSION

To set SHL as an icon for both clients and competitors in the fields of innovation, product quality, service standard, profitability and community work.

MISSION

  • For Our Clients: To offer a global touch, maintaining Bangladeshi culture in living and work places, with utmost uncompromising service to our clients and value for money.
  • For Our Investors: To ensure a smooth upward-reasonable trend of return on investment.
  • For Our Employees: To give employees a feeling of satisfaction by maximizing their potentials and providing means for their personal well-being and career development.

GOAL

Our vision & mission are being made into reality through the belief and implementation of 6 core values. Excellence, Innovation, Commitment, Quality, Valuing People & Customer Satisfaction are embedded in our DNA and drives toward our motto of “setting standards”.

  • Excellence: Excellence in every aspect of each project is how SHL aims to win the hearts of their clients, because at the end of the day – home is where the heart is.
  • Innovation: Continuous improvement and innovation is key to remaining at the top of the industry. SHL aims to set new standards in each project, so having an innovative mindset is what drives them to go further.
  • Commitment: Commitment to their customers and landowners in terms of on-time delivery of their dream projects, with the promised quality and specifications. Commitment to their team members, to maximize their potentials and provide means for their well-being and career development.
  • Quality: Quality is the core guiding principle at SHL, it’s what sets them apart. They aim to set new benchmarks of quality without compromise, both in terms of their products and their customer service.
  • Value People: SHL value the talent, time and intentions of everyone they work with. They take interest in and embrace each other’s individuality, stay true to who they are, stand up for what they believe in, and are always mindful of others.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Ensuring utmost uncompromising service to the clients and value for money is the key of their loyal customer base.
